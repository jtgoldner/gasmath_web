Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

GasMath ("we", "us") helps you find the most cost-effective gas station for your situation. This policy explains what data the app handles. The guiding principle is simple: GasMath has no user accounts and stores no personal data on its servers.

What we store on your device

Your preferences — your vehicle, warehouse-club membership, Top Tier preference, and fuel-grade choice — are saved only in your browser's local storage, on your device. They are never transmitted to or stored on our servers, and we cannot see them. Clearing your browser data removes them.

Location

When you ask GasMath to find a station, your browser requests your location with your permission. Your coordinates are sent to our serverless function only to query nearby gas stations and driving distances for that request. We do not store your location, and we do not associate it with any identity. You can decline or revoke the location permission in your browser at any time.

Third-party services

We rely on these providers to answer your query:

  • Google Maps Platform (Places API) for station locations and live fuel prices. Your use of features powered by Google is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenRouteService for driving-distance calculations.
  • Google Analytics (GA4), if enabled, to understand aggregate, anonymous usage (for example, how many people reach a verdict). We do not use it to identify you. See Google's Privacy Policy.

What we do not do

No accounts, no advertising profiles, no selling of data, no server-side storage of your preferences or location.

Children

GasMath is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their data.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions? Contact us at contact@gasmath.app.
