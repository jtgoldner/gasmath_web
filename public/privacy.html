Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

GasMath ("we", "us") helps you find the most cost-effective gas station for your situation. This policy explains what data the app handles. The guiding principle is simple: GasMath has no user accounts and stores no personal data on its servers.

What we store on your device

Your preferences — your vehicle, warehouse-club membership, Top Tier preference, and fuel-grade choice — plus minor interface state such as which tips you've dismissed, are saved only in your browser's local storage, on your device. They are never transmitted to or stored on our servers, and we cannot see them. Clearing your browser data removes them.

Location

When you ask GasMath to find a station, your browser requests your location with your permission. Your coordinates are sent to our serverless function, which forwards them to Google (to find nearby stations and prices) and to OpenRouteService (to calculate driving distance from you) for that single request. We do not store your location or associate it with any identity, and our function keeps no log of it. You can decline or revoke the location permission in your browser at any time.

Third-party services

We rely on these providers to answer your query:

  • Google Maps Platform (Places API) for station locations and live fuel prices. Your use of features powered by Google is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenRouteService for driving-distance calculations; your coordinates and the candidate station locations are sent to it to compute distances.
  • Google Analytics (GA4), if enabled, to understand aggregate usage (for example, how many people reach a verdict). When enabled, GA4 sets cookies and a pseudonymous identifier in your browser; we use it only for aggregate analytics and never to identify you personally. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Cookies

GasMath sets no cookies of its own — your preferences live in local storage, not cookies. The only cookies that may be set are Google Analytics' own, and only if analytics is enabled (see above).

What we do not do

No accounts, no advertising profiles, no selling of data, no server-side storage of your preferences or location.

Children

GasMath is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their data.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions? Contact us at contact@gasmath.app.
